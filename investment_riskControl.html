  • 风险控制

    Risk control

  • 风控体系

    risk system

    汇熙风控体系由风险控制部门负责，汇熙将投资的风控分为事前、事中、事后三个环节，用汇熙自主研发的风控系统作为执行标准。监测投资部与交易部的可能性风险，对每笔投资与交易进行实时风险监督，将风险严格控制在安全范围内。

    Huixi risk control system is responsible for the risk control department, Huixi investment risk control is divided into before, after, after three links, with Huixi self-developed wind control system as the implementation standard. Monitor the potential risks of the investment and trading department, conduct real-time risk monitoring of each investment and transaction, and strictly control the risk within the scope of safety.

    SAFETY

  • 保密性及合规性

    confidentiality and compliance

    汇熙通过法律协议及强制追责制确保投资环节的信息安全，杜绝信息泄露对投资环节造成的收益损失，对投资人的信息严格保密，确保安全、私密。在合规方面，汇熙设有专业的合规部门与法律团队，合规部门严格把控公司的合规问题，将合法合规贯彻到公司的每步细节，确保公司运营及基金运营的合法合规，杜绝违规风险。

    Huixi through legal agreements and mandatory accountability system to ensure the security of investment links, to prevent the loss of information leakage caused by investment links, investors' information strictly confidential, to ensure safety, privacy. In terms of compliance with professional Huixi compliance department and the legal team, compliance departments to strictly control the company's compliance issues, will carry out legal compliance to the company every step of the details, to ensure legal compliance of the company's operations and the operation of the fund, to prevent illegal risk.
