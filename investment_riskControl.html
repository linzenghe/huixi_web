  • 风控体系

    — risk system

    汇熙风控体系由专门的风险控制负责人，根据投资决策委员会设定的风控标准，进行动态监控、及时提示风险点，并对合规风控委员会所下的指示坚决执行，规避风险。

    Huixi the wind control system controlled by the special risk responsible person, according to the investment decision Committee set the wind control standards, dynamic monitoring, prompt risk, and compliance risk control under the direction of the Committee resolutely implement, to avoid the risk.

  • 保密性及合规性

    — confidentiality and compliance

    团队通过法律协议及强制追责制确保投资环节的信息安全，杜绝信息泄露对投资环节造成的收益损失。

    Investment research team through legal agreements and mandatory accountability system to ensure the security of investment links, to prevent the loss of information on the investment links caused by the loss of income.
