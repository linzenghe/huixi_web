The discerning investment

“ 目睭金金 ” 一词，取自闽南方言，意指 “ 眼光独到 ”

「 MU ZHOU JIN JIN 」is a word from MIN NAN dialect, means discerning and unique perspection .

眼光独到的你 眼光独到的我们

To be a man with unique insight, like us.
